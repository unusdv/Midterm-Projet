About PowerFit

Our mission and our facilities.

Our Mission

At PowerFit Gym, our mission is simple: help every member become healthier, stronger, and more confident. We blend modern training science with old-school discipline so that every workout drives you closer to your goals.

From your first squat to your hundredth marathon, our trainers, classes, and community are here to support you. We believe fitness should be welcoming, sustainable, and most of all - fun.

Our Facilities

Built for serious training, designed for everyone - from first-time members to seasoned athletes.

Cardio Zone

50+ machines including treadmills, ellipticals, rowers, and air bikes - all equipped with personal entertainment screens.

Strength Floor

Olympic lifting platforms, dumbbells up to 50kg, full power racks, and a dedicated free-weight area.

Group Studios

Two large studios for HIIT, yoga, spinning, boxing, and dance classes - 60+ scheduled sessions per week.
